So what are the rules?

Simple. Answer the questions!

I know, I know, you want to know more than that. Well, here you go.

Each round you'll get a series of math problems. We're starting you off easy with adding and subtracting single digit numbers. After that you'll get multiplication and division, still single digits.

As you keep going you'll get a mix of all four types of problems. The longer you go, the harder the questions.

OK, that is pretty simple. How do I play?

To start the game, click the Start button. Type your answer in the box and click "Enter". That's it!

If you got the answer right you'll see your points go up. If you didn't, no problem. You just didnt' get that point. Don't sweat it. Move on to the next one.

Cool! What do I need to win?

101 points. That's all you need. How quickly you can get those points, that's the challenge!
